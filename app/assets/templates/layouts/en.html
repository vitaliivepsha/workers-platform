On March 3, 2022, a law was passed in Germany to help Ukrainian refugees.

According to him, all citizens of Ukraine, all persons with permanent residence in Ukraine and members of their families have the right to a temporary residence permit in Germany (for one year with the possibility of extension for another two years).

This document provides an opportunity to work, study, receive social assistance in Germany.

We want to help all Ukrainians who have found themselves in a difficult situation as a result of Russia's military aggression in Ukraine.
